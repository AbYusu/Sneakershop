About us

SneakerShop is all about footwear. We have been in the shoe business for over 130 years. Yes, you read that correctly, over a century plus a quarter. Today we are proud to offer our best customer service to patrons both online here at SneakerShop and in our shoe stores internationally

Since starting SneakerShop, we have devoted our energy to providing personable and knowledgable customer service with quick free shipping to every customer. Excellent customer service is the standard of Men’s Footwear and SneakerShop that will not be compromised. Our goal is to make sure that your demands of quality, style and comfort are fulfilled.

Style, comfort and quality are the focus of the SneakerShop buying team. The brands we carry are a reflection of our values which is why we search the world for the finest footwear every season. We know that if you don’t take care of your feet then your whole body suffers. Brands such as Nike, adidas, Jordan Brand, adidas Originals, Converse, Reebok, PUMA, New Balance, ASICS, Vans, and many more epitomize what we are about. Top-quality styles that are crafted with support, style and exquisite materials. As a bonus every SneakerShop order is delivered free of charge and with free return shipping too.
